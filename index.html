Fun Fact

One of the most unusual cybersecurity vulnerabilities ever documented confirms that a song can literally be a weapon. The music video for Janet Jackson's 1989 hit "Rhythm Nation" contains a specific resonant frequency that can physically crash certain 5400 RPM laptop hard drives common in the mid-2000s. The acoustic attack was so effective that manufacturers eventually had to add a custom filter to their audio pipeline to remove the offending frequencies and protect the hardware. Learn more.
